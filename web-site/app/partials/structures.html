Structures owned by players on the server

Structure Benefits

Any players who own structure will get a reward package (9 items) and notifications (message in-game and email) when someone enter in there area. If you want your friend to not trigger notifications please ask on the forum.

Actions done by any players inside those specifics area are logged. Any grief will be punished by a server ban.
Any construction done in the perimeter of a referenced structure can be punished by a server ban.

How can I claim a structure?

You have to upload on the forum a photo of your creation with your name and give the location privately.
