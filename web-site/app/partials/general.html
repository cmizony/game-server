Lives

You start with 3 lives and are automatically resurrected the day after in the morning in case of death.
Use the daily Token you receive in game to vote for players, the top 3 will get 1 life each at the end of the week.

Score

The score system is calculated using the achievments system based on their difficulty.
The more you do them the more you get points, up to a certain limit.

Rewards

Get regularely a package of 9 nice items based on your score, every 1,000 points.
You can also build nice structures and get rewarded for it with a package of items.

Structures

You can own structures and get notified in-game and by email when players come to visit it
If you play with friends they can be added on the whitelist to avoid notification for them

Map

A dynamique world map is available on the map tab.
It includes point of interests (like structures) and a real time players actions update.
