Lives

You start with 3 lives and are automatically resurrected the day after in the morning in case of death.
Use the daily Token you receive in game to vote for players, the top 3 will get 1 life each at the end of the week.

Score

The score system is calculated using the achievments system based on their difficulty.
The more you do them the more you get points, up to a certain limit.

Rewards

Get regularely a package of 9 nice items based on your score, every 1,000 points.
You can also build nice structures and get rewarded for it with a package of items.

Structures

You can own structures and get notified in-game and by email when players come to visit it
If you play with friends they can be added on the whitelist to avoid notification for them

Map

A dynamique world map is available when you will have discover all explored biomes.
It includes point of interests (like structures) and a real time players actions update.

Structures owned by players on the server

Structure Benefits

Any players who own structure will get a reward package (9 items) and notifications (message in-game and email) when someone enter in there area. If you want your friend to not trigger notifications please ask on the forum.

Actions done by any players inside those specifics area are logged. Any grief will be punished by a server ban.
Any construction done in the perimeter of a referenced structure can be punished by a server ban.

How can I claim a structure?

You have to upload on the forum a photo of your creation with your name and give the location privately.

World map

The worldmap is rendered periodically every day to keep the content updated. It contains the overworld surface and a layout to hightlight biomes.

The interface as been customized to show points of interests like structures and players. Players location are updated every minute and include their life, food level, experience level and actions.
